Varanasi: Timeless journey of Spirit

Duration:3 Days and 2 Nights

Locations:Varanasi

Varanasi, the ancient spiritual capital of India, enchants visitors with its timeless ghats and sacred rituals. This mystical city, where the sacred Ganges flows alongside centuries-old traditions, invites travelers to immerse themselves in its profound cultural and spiritual heritage. Every moment in Varanasi offers a transformative experience, echoing the eternal dance of life and devotion.

Package Features

  • Premium hotel accommodations
  • Daily breakfast and selected meals
  • Private airport transfers
  • Professional English-speaking drivers
  • All entry tickets
  • Start your day with an early sunrise boat ride, followed by a visit to Kashi Vishwanath Corridor
  • A city tour will be arranged, visiting BHU, Birla Temple, Sankat Mochan Temple, Tulsi Manas Temple, Durga Temple, and Bharat Mata Temple
  • Dive into the local food on the evening walk with the guide
  • Visit the Dashasumedh Ghat for Ganga Aarti
  • An excursion to Ramnagar Fort, Sarnath, and Sarvoday Temple
